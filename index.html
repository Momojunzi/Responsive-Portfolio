About Me

My name is Mark Holthaus. I'm a fullstack web developer from Berkeley California. My passion is making digital instruments and web based tools for live music and digital recording. I also play guitar and write music. This portfolio is shows some of the projects I have worked on. If you like what you see or you would like to collaborate on a digital project or a music project please contact me.


Before I became a web developer I was a business development manager for Penske Truck Leasing in the San Francisco Bay Area. I also hold a masters degree in Traditional Chinese Medicine and practiced as an acupuncturist in San Francisco, CA and Berkeley, CA. I believe my experience in Business management and health services gives my work as a web developer a unique perspective. Thanks for taking the time to look at my profile and I wish you good health!
